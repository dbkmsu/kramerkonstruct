About KramerKonstruct

Independent building with a clear point of view.

Built around efficiency, restraint, and long-term value.

KramerKonstruct is a sole proprietorship serving clients in and around Okemos, Michigan. The work centers on smaller homes that prioritize comfort, lower energy demand, healthier materials, and thoughtful detailing over unnecessary square footage.

That means focusing on what improves daily life: useful layouts, durable assemblies, natural light, practical storage, and materials that age well. The result is a home that feels intentional from the first sketch through the final finish.
